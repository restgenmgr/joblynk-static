💆 Wellness & Spa

Complete Career Guide – Spa Management, Therapists, Wellness Centers

Spa & Wellness Roles & Salaries (Annual INR)

  • Spa Therapist: ₹2.5 – 5 LPA + commissions
  • Senior Therapist: ₹4 – 7 LPA
  • Spa Supervisor: ₹4 – 6 LPA
  • Assistant Spa Manager: ₹5 – 8 LPA
  • Spa Manager: ₹6 – 12 LPA
  • Wellness Director (Luxury Resort): ₹10 – 20 LPA
  • Yoga Instructor: ₹3 – 8 LPA
  • Fitness Trainer: ₹3 – 6 LPA

Required Certifications

  • CIDESCO (International beauty and spa therapy)
  • CIBTAC (UK standard)
  • International Yoga Certification
  • Personal Trainer Certification (ACE, NASM)
  • Ayurveda Therapy Certification (Kerala specific)

Top Employers in India

  • Oberoi Spa – Wildflower Hall, Udaivilas
  • Taj Spa – Jiva Spa brand
  • Leela Spas – Across luxury properties
  • Four Seasons – Mumbai, Bangalore
  • Ananda in the Himalayas – Rishikesh
🌍 International Opportunities: Cruise Lines (Royal Caribbean), Middle East (Atlantis, Burj Al Arab), Maldives luxury resorts.
