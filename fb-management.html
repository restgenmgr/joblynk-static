🍽️ F&B Management

Complete Career Guide – Hotels, Restaurants, Cruise Lines, QSR

Career Roles in Food & Beverage

  • F&B Server / Waiter: Entry-level role in restaurants, banquets, and outlets.
  • Steward / Kitchen Helper: Back-of-house support.
  • F&B Captain / Supervisor: Leads a section, handles guest issues.
  • Restaurant Manager: Full P&L accountability for one outlet.
  • Banquet Manager: Coordinates events, weddings, conferences.
  • F&B Manager: Oversees all F&B outlets, cost control, menu engineering.
  • Food & Beverage Director (Corporate): Multi-property strategy, brand standards.

Salary Guide (Annual in INR)

  • F&B Server / Steward: ₹1.8 – 3 LPA (plus tips)
  • F&B Captain / Supervisor: ₹3 – 5 LPA
  • Restaurant Manager: ₹5 – 10 LPA
  • Banquet Manager: ₹4 – 8 LPA
  • F&B Manager (5‑star hotel): ₹8 – 18 LPA
  • F&B Director: ₹18 – 35 LPA
  • Cruise Line F&B Manager: ₹12 – 25 LPA (tax‑free)
  • QSR Store Manager (Domino’s, McDonald’s, KFC): ₹4 – 8 LPA

Essential Skills & Certifications

  • Menu engineering & food costing
  • Inventory management and vendor negotiation
  • HACCP, ServSafe, or FSSAI food safety certification
  • POS systems (Micros, Toast, Zoho, etc.)
  • Wine & beverage knowledge (WSET certificate is a plus)
  • Team leadership and conflict resolution
  • Sales and upselling techniques
🍷 Pro Tip: A certification in Beverage Management (Wine, Spirits, Cocktails) significantly increases earning potential – many upscale venues pay premium for skilled sommeliers or mixologists.

Top Employers for F&B Professionals

  • Luxury Hotels: Taj, Oberoi, ITC, Leela, Marriott, Hyatt, Four Seasons
  • Fine Dining Chains: Olive Bar & Kitchen, Farzi Café, Karavalli, Indigo Delicatessen
  • QSR & Casual Dining: McDonald’s, KFC, Pizza Hut, Domino’s, Barista, Starbucks, Chaayos
  • Cruise Lines: Royal Caribbean, Carnival, MSC, Norwegian, Disney
  • Contract Catering: Sodexo, Compass Group, Elior India
  • Cloud Kitchens & Aggregators: Rebel Foods, EatSure, Curefoods

Career Progression Path

  • 0-2 years: Entry-level (Server / Steward) – ₹1.8 – 3 LPA
  • 2-4 years: Supervisor / Captain – ₹3 – 5 LPA
  • 4-7 years: Restaurant / Outlet Manager – ₹5 – 10 LPA
  • 7-10 years: Multi-Unit / F&B Manager – ₹8 – 18 LPA
  • 10+ years: Food & Beverage Director – ₹18 – 35 LPA+
💡 Insider Tip: The rise of cloud kitchens and delivery‑only brands has created new roles like "Virtual Restaurant Manager" and "Dark Kitchen Operations Lead" – these positions offer faster growth due to data‑driven decision making.

Where to Find F&B Jobs in India & Middle East

  • Naukri, Indeed, LinkedIn (search for "restaurant manager", "F&B supervisor")
  • Hozpitality (specialised hospitality job portal)
  • Odgers Berndtson, Stanton Chase (executive search for senior roles)
  • Direct company websites: Taj Careers, Oberoi Careers, Emaar Hospitality (Dubai)
  • Walk‑in interviews conducted by hotels and QSR chains
