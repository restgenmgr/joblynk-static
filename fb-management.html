🍽️ F&B Management – Career Path

Entry Level

  • F&B Associate / Steward
  • Host / Hostess
  • Commi Waiter
💰 ₹1.8 – 3 LPA

Mid Level

  • F&B Supervisor
  • Captain / Senior Waiter
  • Restaurant Shift Manager
💰 ₹3 – 6 LPA

Senior Level

  • Restaurant Manager
  • F&B Manager
  • Banquet Manager
💰 ₹6 – 15 LPA

📋 Key Skills for F&B Management

  • ✅ Customer service & guest handling
  • ✅ Food safety & hygiene (HACCP, FSSAI knowledge)
  • ✅ Inventory management & cost control
  • ✅ Staff scheduling & training
  • ✅ Basic accounting & billing systems (POS)

🌍 Where F&B Professionals Work

🏨 Hotels

Fine dining, coffee shops, banquets, room service, bars

🚢 Cruise Ships

Main dining room, specialty restaurants, buffet, crew mess

🍽️ Standalone Restaurants

Casual dining, QSR, fine dining, cafe chains
