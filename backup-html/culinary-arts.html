👨‍🍳 Culinary Arts

Complete Career Guide – Hotels, Restaurants, Cruise Lines, QSR

Kitchen Career Roles & Salaries (Annual INR)

  • Commis (Fresher): ₹2 – 3.5 LPA
  • Chef de Partie (CDP): ₹4 – 8 LPA
  • Sous Chef: ₹6 – 12 LPA
  • Executive Sous Chef: ₹10 – 18 LPA
  • Executive Chef (5-star): ₹15 – 40 LPA
  • Corporate Chef: ₹30 – 60 LPA
  • Executive Chef (Cruise Line): ₹25 – 50 LPA (tax-free)

Essential Kitchen Skills

  • Knife handling and butchery skills
  • Food costing and portion control
  • Inventory management (FIFO system)
  • HACCP and food safety compliance
  • Menu planning and recipe standardization
  • Team management and training

Top Culinary Institutes in India

  • IHM Mumbai – Premier institute
  • IHM Delhi – Pusa
  • Welcomgroup WGSHA Manipal
  • Culinary Academy of India (CAI), Hyderabad
  • Le Cordon Bleu – New Delhi campus
💡 Pro Tip: A specialization in Pastry or Bakery can lead to faster career growth – demand exceeds supply.
