Retail Roles & Salaries (Annual INR)

  • Retail Sales Associate: ₹2.5 – 4 LPA + commissions
  • Boutique Manager: ₹5 – 10 LPA
  • Duty-Free Supervisor (Airport/Resort): ₹4 – 7 LPA
  • Retail Operations Manager: ₹8 – 15 LPA

Casino Roles & Salaries

  • Casino Manager: ₹10 – 20 LPA
  • Slot Technician: ₹3 – 5 LPA
  • Security - Casino Floor: ₹3 – 5 LPA
