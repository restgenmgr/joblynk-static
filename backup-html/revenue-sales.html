📊 Revenue & Sales

Complete Career Guide – Hotels, Resorts, Hospitality Groups

Revenue & Sales Roles & Salaries (Annual INR)

  • Sales Coordinator: ₹3 – 5 LPA
  • Sales Executive: ₹4 – 7 LPA + incentives
  • Sales Manager (Corporate): ₹6 – 12 LPA + incentives
  • Revenue Analyst: ₹5 – 9 LPA
  • Revenue Manager: ₹8 – 18 LPA
  • Director of Sales: ₹15 – 30 LPA
  • Regional Director of Sales: ₹25 – 50 LPA

Key Responsibilities

  • Room inventory management and pricing strategy
  • Corporate account acquisition and retention
  • Group bookings and MICE sales
  • Competitor rate analysis and market positioning
  • Channel management (OTA, direct, GDS)
  • Sales forecasting and budgeting

Essential Skills & Certifications

  • PMS expertise (Opera, IDS)
  • Revenue Management Certification (Cornell, AHLA)
  • CRM software proficiency (Salesforce, Oracle)
  • Data analysis and Excel advanced skills
  • Negotiation and relationship management
