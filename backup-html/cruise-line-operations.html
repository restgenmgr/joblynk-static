Complete Career Guide for Indian Seafarers | Jobs, Salaries, Recruitment Agents

Overview of the Cruise Industry in India

The global cruise industry is experiencing a remarkable resurgence, with India emerging as a key source market for cruise line talent. Major cruise lines including Carnival, Royal Caribbean, Norwegian Cruise Line, MSC Cruises, and Disney Cruise Line actively recruit Indian professionals for shipboard positions across hotel operations, food & beverage, guest services, and deck departments.

Major Port Cities for Cruise Recruitment in India

  • Mumbai (Maharashtra) – India's cruise capital, home to Mumbai Port Trust. Key agents: Anglo-Eastern, Synergy Maritime, V.Ships.
  • Cochin (Kerala) – Growing cruise hub with international connectivity. Key agents: Cochin Shipyard, Southern Shipping.
  • Chennai (Tamil Nadu) – Strategic port for east coast operations. Key agents: Fleet Management, Wilhelmsen.
  • Goa (Mormugao) – Popular for crew changeovers and smaller cruise operations.
  • Kolkata (West Bengal) – Emerging market for river cruise operations.

Top Maritime Recruitment Agents in India

  • Anglo-Eastern Ship Management (Mumbai, Cochin, Chennai)
  • Synergy Maritime (Mumbai, Chennai)
  • V.Ships India (Mumbai, Cochin)
  • Fleet Management (Mumbai, Cochin)
  • Wilhelmsen Ship Management (Mumbai)

Salary Ranges for Cruise Ship Positions (Monthly in INR)

  • Deck Cadet: ₹50,000 – ₹80,000
  • Engine Cadet: ₹50,000 – ₹80,000
  • F&B Server: ₹60,000 – ₹1,20,000 (including tips)
  • Housekeeping Attendant: ₹50,000 – ₹90,000
  • Guest Services Officer: ₹80,000 – ₹1,50,000
  • Cruise Director: ₹1,50,000 – ₹3,00,000
  • Hotel Director: ₹3,00,000 – ₹6,00,000
📌 Pro Tip: Start with a European cruise line (MSC, Costa, AIDA) as they have fewer requirements for Indian crew. Once you have 6-12 months of experience, American brands (Royal Caribbean, Carnival, Norwegian) become accessible with higher earning potential.

Required Certifications for Indian Seafarers

  • STCW Basic Safety Training (mandatory)
  • CDC (Continuous Discharge Certificate) from DG Shipping
  • Seafarer's Medical Certificate (from DG Shipping approved doctors)
  • Passport with minimum 18 months validity
  • USPH / ServSafe certification (for hotel department roles)

Training Institutes in India for Cruise Careers

  • IMTC (International Maritime Training Centre) – Mumbai, Cochin, Chennai
  • AMET University – Chennai
  • Tolani Maritime Institute – Pune
  • Samundra Institute of Maritime Studies – Mumbai
  • Maritime Training Academy – Kolkata
