⚙️ Maritime Engine Dept

Complete Career Guide – Marine Engineers, Engine Ratings

Engine Department Roles & Salaries (Monthly in USD/INR)

  • Engine Cadet: $500 – 800 (₹40,000 – 65,000)
  • Fourth Engineer: $2,500 – 3,500 (₹2 – 3 Lakhs)
  • Third Engineer: $3,500 – 5,000 (₹3 – 4 Lakhs)
  • Second Engineer: $6,000 – 8,000 (₹5 – 7 Lakhs)
  • Chief Engineer: $10,000 – 16,000 (₹8 – 13 Lakhs)
  • Oiler / Motorman: $1,200 – 1,800 (₹1 – 1.5 Lakhs)
  • Fitter: $1,000 – 1,500 (₹80,000 – 1.2 Lakhs)

Required Qualifications

  • BE / B.Tech Marine Engineering (4 years)
  • MEO Class IV, III, II, I certifications (DG Shipping)
  • STCW Basic Safety Training
  • CDC and Seafarer's Medical Certificate
  • ECR (Emigration Check Required)

Top Marine Engineering Institutes in India

  • AMET University – Chennai
  • Tolani Maritime Institute – Pune
  • Samundra Institute – Mumbai
  • IMU (Indian Maritime University) – Multiple campuses
  • Cochin University of Science & Technology
💰 Salary Note: Chief Engineer salaries on LNG/LPG tankers can reach $16,000 – $20,000 per month.
