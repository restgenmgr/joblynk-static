What is an ATS and Why Does it Matter?

An Applicant Tracking System (ATS) scans, parses, and ranks resumes based on keywords and formatting. Over 90% of top hospitality employers — including Marriott, Hyatt, OYO, Nexottel Group of Hotels — use ATS to shortlist candidates.

? Pro Tip: ATS-friendly ? boring. It means clean, readable, and optimized for both robots and recruiters.

2. Keyword Optimization for Hospitality Roles

  • Operations Manager: Revenue management, guest satisfaction, SOP implementation, P&L management
  • Food & Beverage Manager: F&B cost control, menu engineering, vendor management, hygiene compliance
  • Front Office Manager: Check-in systems, guest relations, OTA management, upselling
  • Housekeeping Manager: Linen inventory, turnover time, quality audit, deep cleaning schedules
