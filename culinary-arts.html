👨‍🍳 Culinary Career Path – Kitchen Brigade

Entry Level

  • Kitchen Assistant / Helper
  • Commi Chef III
  • Commi Chef II
💰 ₹1.8 – 3 LPA (India) | $800–1,200/month (Cruise)

Mid Level

  • Commi Chef I
  • Chef de Partie (CDP)
  • Demi Chef de Partie
💰 ₹3.5 – 7 LPA (India) | $1,500–2,500/month (Cruise)

Senior Level

  • Sous Chef
  • Executive Sous Chef
  • Executive Chef
💰 ₹8 – 25 LPA (India) | $3,000–7,000/month (Cruise)

📋 Specializations in Culinary Arts

🍝 Continental

Italian, French, Mediterranean, American cuisine

🍜 Asian

Chinese, Thai, Japanese, Korean, Indonesian

🍛 Indian

North Indian, South Indian, Tandoor, Mughlai, Street Food

🍰 Bakery & Patisserie

Breads, pastries, desserts, wedding cakes, chocolates

🌍 Where Chefs Work

  • ✅ Hotels: All star categories – kitchen is the heart
  • ✅ Cruise Ships: Massive demand for Indian chefs of all levels
  • ✅ Standalone Restaurants: Fine dining, QSR, cafes, cloud kitchens
  • ✅ Middle East: Dubai, Qatar, Saudi – tax-free salaries
  • ✅ Germany: Skilled worker visa for chefs – €2,500–4,000/month

📋 Requirements for Cruise Ship Chefs

  • ✅ 1–3 years kitchen experience (hotel or restaurant)
  • ✅ STCW Basic Safety Training certificate
  • ✅ Food safety & hygiene certification (HACCP)
  • ✅ Valid Indian passport + C1/D visa
