Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 29, 2026

Welcome to JobLynk.live. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Fill out our contact form (name, email address, phone number, message)
  • Subscribe to our newsletter (email address)
  • Purchase a career package (name, email, phone, payment details – processed securely via third-party gateways)
  • Communicate with us via email or WhatsApp

We do not collect sensitive personal data unless you explicitly provide it.

2. Automatically Collected Information

When you visit JobLynk.live, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website (if you came from a search engine or link)

This information is collected via Google Analytics and is used solely to improve our website and understand visitor behaviour. It is anonymised and never sold to third parties.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, analyse site traffic, and serve personalised ads through Google AdSense. By using our site, you consent to our use of cookies.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect certain features of our website.

4. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics may collect information such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on site. This data is anonymised and used solely for statistical purposes. You can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on to prevent data collection.

6. How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Process your career package orders
  • Send you updates, promotions, or service-related emails (only if you opt in)
  • Improve our website content and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

7. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only:

  • With service providers who assist us in operating our website (e.g., Formsubmit for contact forms, payment gateways) – these parties are bound by confidentiality agreements

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to external websites (e.g., WhatsApp, LinkedIn). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

11. Your Rights

To exercise these rights, please contact us at joblynklive@gmail.com.
