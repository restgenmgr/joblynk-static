📋 Requirements for Indian Candidates

  • ✅ Valid Indian passport (minimum 6 months validity after visa)
  • ✅ Hotel management degree or diploma (preferred)
  • ✅ 1–3 years experience in 3/4/5-star hotels (freshers limited roles)
  • ✅ Good English communication skills (spoken & written)
  • ✅ Police clearance certificate (for work visa)
  • ✅ Medical fitness test (for visa stamping)

🔄 How to Apply from India

Step 1: Update your CV with recent photo and experience details

Step 2: Apply on job portals: NaukriGulf, LinkedIn, Bayt, Indeed UAE

Step 3: Register with recruitment agencies (MMI India, Careers International, etc.)

Step 4: Attend virtual interviews on Zoom/Teams

Step 5: Get job offer → Visa processing (2–6 weeks) → Fly to Middle East
