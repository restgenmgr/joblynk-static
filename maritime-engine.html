⚙️ Engine Department – Career Path

Entry Level

  • Engine Cadet (Training)
  • Fitter / Mechanic (Rating)
💰 Stipend: $400–700/month

Mid Level

  • Fourth Engineer (4th Eng)
  • Third Engineer (3rd Eng)
  • Second Engineer (2nd Eng)
💰 $3,000 – $8,000/month

Senior Level

  • Chief Engineer
  • Technical Superintendent
💰 $9,000 – $18,000/month

📋 Requirements for Engine Cadet

  • ✅ B.E. / B.Tech in Marine Engineering
  • ✅ 10+2 with PCM – minimum 60%
  • ✅ Age: 17–25 years
  • ✅ IMU CET or sponsorship from shipping company
  • ✅ Approved pre-sea training institute
  • ✅ STCW basic safety certificates

🌍 Entry Routes to Engine Career

  • ✅ B.Tech Marine Engineering (4 years)
  • ✅ Diploma in Marine Engineering (3 years) followed by B.Tech lateral entry
  • ✅ GME (Graduate Marine Engineering) – 1 year course after B.E. Mechanical
  • ✅ Sponsorship from companies like Anglo-Eastern, Maersk, V-Ships, Synergy, etc.
