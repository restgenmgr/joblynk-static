🎤 Hospitality Interview Tips (2026)

Walk into your next hotel, F&B or cruise line interview fully prepared.

Research the Property

Read recent TripAdvisor reviews, the brand's service standards, and any recent news about the property before your interview. Reference something specific — it signals genuine interest.

Master the STAR Method

Answer every behavioural question with: Situation, Task, Action, Result. Prepare 5 stories covering: guest complaints, difficult team members, cost-saving initiatives, high-pressure periods, and service recovery.

💡 Most asked: "Tell me about a time you turned an unhappy guest into a loyal one." Have your best story ready.

Show Commercial Awareness

Be ready to discuss RevPAR, GOPPAR, covers targets, food cost %, payroll ratios, and upselling revenue. Weave these into answers naturally.

Smart Questions to Ask

  • "What does success look like in the first 90 days?"
  • "What are the biggest service challenges the team faces?"
  • "How do you measure guest satisfaction and where is it trending?"
  • "What growth opportunities exist within the group?"

Follow Up Within 24 Hours

A brief, specific thank-you note referencing one thing discussed sets you apart from 90% of candidates.
