📈 Hospitality Jobs in India 2026

India's hospitality sector is in a significant expansion phase. Here's where the opportunities are and how to position yourself.

Where Growth Is Happening

  • Tier-2 cities: Ahmedabad, Pune, Kochi, Coimbatore, Indore and Jaipur seeing significant new property openings
  • Luxury segment: IHG, Marriott, Hyatt and IHCL all in active expansion with pre-opening opportunities
  • F&B concepts: Standalone restaurants and QSR expansion creating new mid-to-senior roles
  • Managed accommodation: Serviced apartments and co-living operators hiring experienced hospitality managers
💡 Where to look beyond job boards: LinkedIn is where most senior hospitality hiring happens in India. WhatsApp groups within hospitality alumni networks remain highly active for mid-senior roles.

What Employers Prioritise in 2026

  • Multi-brand or multi-property experience
  • Demonstrated P&L management at scale
  • Pre-opening experience for the expanding Tier-2 pipeline
  • Technology comfort — PMS, POS, analytics platforms
  • Bilingual communication for properties with international guest mix

How to Accelerate Your Search

Senior hospitality roles are rarely advertised widely — most are filled through networks or executive search. Keep your LinkedIn active, reconnect with former colleagues now in senior positions, and approach Hotel GMs and F&B Directors directly with a well-crafted message rather than applying cold through job boards.

Sectors Hiring Aggressively Now

  • Hospital hospitality divisions
  • Corporate cafeteria management companies
  • Airline catering
  • Premium retail food concepts
