💰 Hospitality Salary Guide India (2026)

Real CTC ranges and negotiation strategies for hospitality professionals across India.

Salary Ranges by Level (Tier-1 Cities)

  • Supervisor / Team Leader: ₹3.5 – 6 LPA
  • Assistant Manager / Outlet Manager: ₹6 – 12 LPA
  • Department Manager: ₹12 – 22 LPA
  • Senior Manager / Director: ₹22 – 40 LPA
  • Director of F&B / Rooms Division: ₹35 – 60 LPA
  • General Manager (mid-scale): ₹45 – 80 LPA
  • General Manager (luxury/5-star): ₹80 LPA – ₹1.5 Cr+
💡 Always calculate net take-home. CTC includes accommodation, meals, transport and PF — compare the true value, not just the headline number.

What's Negotiable Beyond Base

  • Accommodation allowance or in-property accommodation
  • Performance bonus structure and KPIs
  • Joining bonus and relocation allowance
  • Medical insurance scope (family coverage)
  • Leave encashment policy

When to Negotiate

After a verbal offer is made — never before. If asked early, give a range: "Based on my research I'm targeting ₹X–Y CTC, though I'm open to discussion based on the full package."

How to Anchor High

Ask for 20–30% above current CTC for a new company move. Internal promotions allow 15–25%. Back your ask with market data, quantified achievements, and the scope increase the new role represents.
