📋 Requirements for Indian Candidates

  • ✅ Valid Indian passport (minimum 2 years validity)
  • ✅ STCW Basic Safety Training certificate (mandatory)
  • ✅ Seaman's Book (CDC – Continuous Discharge Certificate)
  • ✅ Medical fitness certificate (seafarer medical)
  • ✅ C1/D visa (for joining ships outside India)
  • ✅ Hotel / hospitality experience preferred for guest-facing roles
