👥 HR & Training

Complete Career Guide – Hospitality Human Resources

HR & Training Roles & Salaries (Annual INR)

  • HR Executive: ₹3 – 5 LPA
  • Recruitment Specialist: ₹4 – 7 LPA
  • Training Coordinator: ₹3.5 – 6 LPA
  • HR Manager: ₹6 – 12 LPA
  • Training Manager: ₹6 – 12 LPA
  • Talent Acquisition Manager: ₹7 – 15 LPA
  • Regional HR Manager: ₹12 – 25 LPA
  • Director of HR (5-star): ₹18 – 35 LPA

Key Responsibilities

  • Recruitment and onboarding
  • Employee engagement and retention
  • Training needs analysis and program delivery
  • Performance management systems
  • Payroll and compliance (PF, ESI, labour laws)
  • HR analytics and reporting
📌 Essential HR Certifications: SHRM-CP / SHRM-SCP, CIPD Level 3/5, Labour Law Certification (India specific), Six Sigma.

Top Hotel Chains Hiring HR Professionals

  • Taj Hotels (IHCL), Oberoi, ITC, Marriott, Hyatt, Hilton, Accor, Leela
