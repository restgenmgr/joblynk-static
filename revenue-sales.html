📊 Revenue Management – Career Path

Entry Level

  • Revenue Analyst
  • Reservations Associate
  • Pricing Coordinator
💰 ₹3 – 5 LPA

Mid Level

  • Revenue Manager
  • Senior Revenue Analyst
  • Distribution Manager
💰 ₹6 – 12 LPA

Senior Level

  • Director of Revenue
  • Regional Revenue Director
  • VP Revenue Management
💰 ₹15 – 35 LPA+

📈 Sales – Career Path in Hospitality

Entry Level

  • Sales Coordinator
  • Sales Executive
  • Corporate Sales Associate
💰 ₹2.5 – 4.5 LPA

Mid Level

  • Assistant Sales Manager
  • Sales Manager (Corporate/MICE)
  • Key Account Manager
💰 ₹5 – 10 LPA

Senior Level

  • Director of Sales
  • Regional Sales Director
  • VP Sales & Marketing
💰 ₹12 – 30 LPA+

📋 Key Skills for Revenue & Sales

Revenue Skills

PMS (Opera, IDS), Excel, data analysis, forecasting, pricing strategy, OTA management.

Sales Skills

Client relationship, negotiation, CRM, proposal writing, event sales, B2B networking.

🌍 Where Revenue & Sales Professionals Work

  • ✅ Hotel Chains: Corporate office or property level
  • ✅ Cruise Lines: Revenue management teams in Mumbai or overseas
  • ✅ Hospitality Tech Companies: OTA (MakeMyTrip, Agoda), RMS providers
  • ✅ Consulting Firms: Hospitality revenue consulting
