⚓ Deck Department – Career Path

Entry Level

  • Deck Cadet (Training)
  • Ordinary Seaman (OS)
💰 Stipend: $300–600/month

Mid Level

  • Third Officer (3rd Mate)
  • Second Officer (2nd Mate)
💰 $2,500 – $5,000/month

Senior Level

  • Chief Officer
  • Captain / Master
💰 $6,000 – $15,000/month

📋 Requirements for Deck Cadet

  • ✅ 10+2 with PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics) – minimum 60%
  • ✅ Age: 17–25 years
  • ✅ Join a DG Shipping approved institute (e.g., TS Chanakya, IMU)
  • ✅ Complete pre-sea training (6–12 months)
  • ✅ STCW basic safety certificates
  • ✅ Medical fitness (seafarer medical standards)

🌍 Entry Routes to Deck Career

  • ✅ IMU CET (Indian Maritime University Common Entrance Test)
  • ✅ DNS (Diploma in Nautical Science) – 1 year followed by sea training
  • ✅ B.Sc. Nautical Science – 3 year degree program
  • ✅ Sponsored by shipping companies (Great Eastern, Anglo-Eastern, etc.)
