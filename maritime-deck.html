⚓ Maritime Deck Dept

Complete Career Guide – Deck Officers, Ratings, Cruise Lines

Deck Department Roles & Salaries (Monthly in USD/INR)

  • Deck Cadet: $500 – 800 (₹40,000 – 65,000)
  • Third Officer: $2,500 – 3,500 (₹2 – 3 Lakhs)
  • Second Officer: $3,500 – 5,000 (₹3 – 4 Lakhs)
  • Chief Officer: $6,000 – 8,000 (₹5 – 7 Lakhs)
  • Captain / Master: $10,000 – 15,000 (₹8 – 12 Lakhs)
  • Boatswain (Bosun): $1,500 – 2,500 (₹1.2 – 2 Lakhs)
  • Able Seaman (AB): $1,200 – 1,800 (₹1 – 1.5 Lakhs)

Required Certifications

  • STCW Basic Safety Training
  • CDC (Continuous Discharge Certificate)
  • Seafarer's Medical Certificate (DG Shipping approved)
  • ECR (Emigration Check Required) – for certain ranks
  • Bridge Resource Management (BRM)
  • ARPA / Radar Navigation Certification

Training Institutes in India

  • IMTC – Mumbai, Cochin, Chennai
  • AMET University – Chennai
  • Tolani Maritime Institute – Pune
  • Samundra Institute – Mumbai
  • Maritime Training Academy – Kolkata
📌 Pro Tip: Start with European cruise lines (MSC, Costa) – they have lower entry requirements for Indian deck cadets.
