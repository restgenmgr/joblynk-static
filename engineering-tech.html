🔧 Engineering Career Path

Entry Level

  • Maintenance Assistant
  • Electrician Helper
  • HVAC Technician Trainee
💰 ₹1.8 – 3 LPA

Mid Level

  • Maintenance Engineer
  • Electrical Supervisor
  • HVAC Technician
💰 ₹3.5 – 6 LPA

Senior Level

  • Chief Engineer (Hotel)
  • Assistant Chief Engineer (Cruise)
  • Engineering Manager
💰 ₹7 – 15 LPA (Hotel) | $3,000–6,000/month (Cruise)

📋 Key Skills for Engineering Roles

Technical Skills

Electrical, plumbing, HVAC, carpentry, painting, building maintenance.

Certifications

ITI (Electrical/Fitter), Diploma in Mechanical/Electrical Engineering.

🌍 Where Engineers Work

  • ✅ Hotels: Maintenance department – always in demand
  • ✅ Cruise Ships: Engineering team (engine room, electrical, HVAC)
  • ✅ Facility Management Companies: Corporate building maintenance
  • ✅ Maritime: Engine Cadet, Junior Engineer (requires additional certifications)
