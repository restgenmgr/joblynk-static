🔧 Engineering & Tech

Complete Career Guide – Hotel Maintenance, Facilities, IT Systems

Engineering Roles & Salaries (Annual INR)

  • Maintenance Technician: ₹2.5 – 4 LPA
  • HVAC Technician: ₹3 – 5 LPA
  • Electrician: ₹2.5 – 4.5 LPA
  • Plumber: ₹2.5 – 4 LPA
  • Chief Engineer (Mid-size): ₹6 – 10 LPA
  • Chief Engineer (Luxury): ₹10 – 18 LPA
  • Director of Engineering: ₹18 – 30 LPA

IT & Tech Roles in Hospitality

  • IT Support Executive: ₹3 – 5 LPA
  • Network Administrator: ₹4 – 7 LPA
  • PMS Administrator (Opera, IDS): ₹5 – 9 LPA
  • Cybersecurity Specialist: ₹6 – 12 LPA
  • IT Manager: ₹6 – 12 LPA
📌 Pro Tip: Hotels value engineers who can speak guest-facing English – communication skills lead to faster promotion to Chief Engineer.

Required Certifications

  • HVAC Certification (ASHRAE)
  • Electrical License (State/City requirement)
  • Fire Safety Certification
  • PMS Certification (Oracle Opera)
  • CompTIA / Cisco (for IT roles)
