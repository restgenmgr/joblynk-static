About JobLynk.live

Executive Digital Resources for the Modern Indian Professional

📌 Our Mission

JobLynk.live is a premier digital platform dedicated to bridging the gap between executive talent and career opportunities in the hospitality and maritime industries across India, the Middle East, and international markets.

👤 Founder & Vision

Founded by hospitality executive Nigel Thomas, our mission is to provide Indian professionals with comprehensive career guides, industry insights, salary benchmarks, and direct connections to top employers. With over 20 years of global leadership experience, Nigel has curated resources that help job seekers navigate complex recruitment landscapes.
