About JobLynk.live

Bridging the gap between executive talent and career opportunities in hospitality and maritime industries

🎯 Our Mission

At JobLynk.live, we bridge the gap between employers, HR managers, and candidates. We help candidates prepare, upskill, and become better equipped when searching for jobs. Our platform empowers professionals with the knowledge, tools, and confidence to succeed in competitive interviews and career transitions.

Who We Are

JobLynk.live is a premier digital platform dedicated to bridging the gap between executive talent and career opportunities in the hospitality and maritime industries across India, the Middle East, and international markets.

Founded by hospitality executive Nigel Thomas, our mission is to provide Indian professionals with comprehensive career guides, industry insights, salary benchmarks, and direct connections to top employers.

What We Do

We at JobLynk.live want to bridge the gap between the employer/HR managers and the candidates. We help candidates to prepare and be better equipped when searching for jobs. Whether you're a fresh graduate or an experienced executive, we provide the resources you need to succeed.
