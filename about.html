Our Mission

JobLynk.live was founded with a single purpose: to give Indian hospitality and maritime professionals access to the same quality of career intelligence, salary benchmarks, and job search resources that were previously available only to candidates in global markets.

For employers and HR managers, we provide a platform where hospitality-specific talent is already prepared, informed, and ready to engage — reducing time-to-hire and improving the quality of candidate conversations from the first interaction.

We believe every professional deserves clear guidance on how to grow their career, what they should be earning, and how to present themselves to the right employers. Our platform delivers that — for free.

Our Story

JobLynk.live was founded by Nigel Anthony Thomas, a hospitality executive with over 25 years of experience across F&B operations, hotel management, and multi-unit business leadership in India and internationally.

Having navigated every level of the hospitality industry — from outlet supervisor to senior F&B leadership — Nigel recognised a consistent gap on both sides of the hiring equation: talented candidates were underselling themselves and missing opportunities, while employers were struggling to find candidates who genuinely understood the industry.

JobLynk.live was built to close that gap — to be the bridge that makes it easier for employers and HR managers to find the right people, and for candidates to secure the jobs they deserve.
