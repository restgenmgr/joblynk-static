🧹 Housekeeping – Career Path

Entry Level

  • Housekeeping Associate
  • Room Attendant
  • Public Area Cleaner
💰 ₹1.8 – 2.8 LPA

Mid Level

  • Housekeeping Supervisor
  • Floor Supervisor
  • Linen Room Incharge
💰 ₹3 – 5 LPA

Senior Level

  • Assistant Housekeeper
  • Executive Housekeeper
  • Director of Housekeeping
💰 ₹6 – 15 LPA

📋 Key Skills for Housekeeping

Technical

Cleaning techniques, chemical handling, linen management, inventory control.

Soft Skills

Attention to detail, time management, physical stamina, teamwork.

🌍 Housekeeping Jobs – Where to Apply

  • ✅ Hotels: All star categories – housekeeping is always hiring
  • ✅ Cruise Ships: High demand for Indian housekeeping staff
  • ✅ Hospitals & Healthcare: Medical facility cleaning
  • ✅ Corporate Offices: Facility management companies
