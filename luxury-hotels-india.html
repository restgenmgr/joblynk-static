🎓 How to Get a Luxury Hotel Job

  • ✓ Complete Hotel Management (IHM) or Diploma in Hospitality
  • ✓ Do a 6-month internship at a 5-star property
  • ✓ Apply directly via hotel career pages (Taj, Oberoi, Marriott careers)
  • ✓ Use LinkedIn and job portals (Naukri, Indeed, Monster)
  • ✓ Learn English communication skills (spoken & written)
