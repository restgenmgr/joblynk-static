🏨 Luxury Hotel Careers in India

Top Hotel Chains • Salary Ranges • Career Progression • Training Institutes

Top Luxury Hotel Chains in India

  • Taj Hotels (IHCL) – Over 100 properties including Taj Mahal Palace Mumbai, Rambagh Palace Jaipur
  • Oberoi Hotels & Resorts – Oberoi Udaivilas, Oberoi Amarvilas (Agra), Oberoi Wildflower Hall
  • ITC Hotels – ITC Maurya (Delhi), ITC Grand Chola (Chennai), ITC Gardenia (Bangalore)
  • Leela Palaces & Resorts – Leela Palace Udaipur, Leela Palace New Delhi
  • Marriott International – JW Marriott, Ritz-Carlton, St. Regis in major cities
  • Hyatt Hotels – Park Hyatt, Grand Hyatt in metropolitan cities

Salary Guide (Annual in INR)

  • Front Office Executive: ₹2.5 – 4 LPA
  • Guest Relations Executive: ₹3 – 5 LPA
  • Duty Manager: ₹4 – 7 LPA
  • Front Office Manager: ₹6 – 12 LPA
  • F&B Server: ₹2 – 3.5 LPA (plus tips)
  • Restaurant Manager: ₹6 – 12 LPA
  • Executive Chef: ₹15 – 40 LPA
  • General Manager: ₹30 – 80 LPA

Top Hotel Management Institutes in India

  • IHM Mumbai – Premier institute
  • IHM Delhi – Pusa
  • IHM Chennai
  • IHM Bangalore
  • WGSHA Manipal
  • Christ University Bangalore
📌 Entrance Exams: NCHMCT JEE (for IHMs), IIHM eCHAT, and university-specific entrance tests. Apply 6-8 months in advance.

Career Progression Path

  • Entry Level (0-2 years): ₹2.5 – 4 LPA
  • Supervisory (2-5 years): ₹4 – 7 LPA
  • Managerial (5-10 years): ₹8 – 15 LPA
  • Executive (10-15 years): ₹18 – 40 LPA
  • Corporate (15+ years): ₹50 LPA+
